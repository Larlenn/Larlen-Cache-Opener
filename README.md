## What Does It Do?
This addon provides a completely effortless way to open various rewards across World of Warcraft. 

You will no longer need to dig through your messy bags looking for Profession Knowledge, Drake Customizations, Adventurer Caches, Calling rewards, Rewards Chests, or Caches.

**How it works:** The addon creates a movable, dynamic action button on your screen that only appears when you actually have something in your bags to open. If you have multiple different items to open, it will neatly generate a row of up to 20 buttons for your opening needs. 

Just click the pop-up button, get your loot, and the button disappears!

## What's New in This Version? 
We took the amazing core functionality and completely overhauled the user experience with a functional Options menu and heavy Quality Of Life improvements. 

Here are some of the brand-new features:
* **Custom Item Support:** You are no longer restricted to hardcoded items! You can now type `/lco add <ItemID>` or use the Options menu to track any openable item in the entire game.
* **Audio Alerts:** Added the ability to play a notification sound when a cache appears, including native WoW pings and BigWigs/DBM style alerts.
* **Minimap Icon:** Added a fully draggable minimap button (fully compatible with ElvUI and custom minimaps) for quick access to your settings.
* **Visual Customization:** You can now add an Action Button Glow to the caches, tweak their transparency (Alpha), and manually fine-tune their exact X/Y coordinates.
* **Test Mode:** A new "Test Icons" button in the options menu allows you to spawn dummy icons so you can perfectly position your layout without needing actual caches in your inventory.

## 💬 Chat Commands

* `/lco` - List of all commands
* `/lco options` - Opens the brand new settings panel
* `/lco add <id>` - Adds a custom item ID to your tracker
* `/lco remove <id>` - Removes a custom item ID from your tracker
* `/lco ignore <id>` - Blacklists an item so it stops appearing
* `/lco unignore <id>` - Removes an item from your blacklist
* `/lco minimap` - Toggles the minimap icon on or off
* `/lco reset` - Wipes all settings and resets the button position to the center of your screen
